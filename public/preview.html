About

Where Deep Expertise
Meets Bold Innovation

I'm Dr. Yoav Shechter — a Government Affairs strategist, AI innovator, and digital architect with decades of hands-on leadership at the highest levels of global regulatory strategy.

My work begins where most consulting stops: at the intersection of systematic, measurable government affairs and the transformative power of artificial intelligence. I don't believe public policy is "not really a profession" — I believe it should be among the most rigorous, data-driven, and strategically managed functions in any organization.

From leading 40+ government affairs professionals at Merck & Co. across 100+ countries, to founding Pharma Israel, to building and running Israel's largest policy leadership forum — my career has been defined by building systems that deliver measurable results.
